## Overview

This repository documents an undergraduate research project investigating the relationship between physical activity levels and musculoskeletal pain among undergraduate students at Nnamdi Azikiwe University (UNIZIK), Nnewi Campus. The study aims to contribute evidence on physical activity and musculoskeletal health in a Nigerian university population — a context underrepresented in existing literature.

---

## Research Details

| Item | Detail |
|------|--------|
| **Study Design** | Cross-sectional observational study |
| **Setting** | Nnamdi Azikiwe University, Nnewi Campus, Nigeria |
| **Target Population** | Undergraduate students |
| **Estimated Sample Size** | ~420 participants |
| **Sampling Method** | Proportional and systematic random sampling |
| **Current Stage** | Protocol / Proposal |

---

## Objectives

- To assess the physical activity levels of undergraduate students at UNIZIK, Nnewi Campus
- To determine the prevalence and distribution of musculoskeletal pain among this population
- To examine the association between physical activity level and musculoskeletal pain

---

## Instruments

| Instrument | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| **IPAQ Short Form (IPAQ-SF)** | Assessment of physical activity level |
| **Nordic Musculoskeletal Questionnaire (NMQ)** | Assessment of musculoskeletal pain by body region |
